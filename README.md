## About Me

Most people wait for the right moment.
I'm building it.

I grew up in **El Salvador** — a country the world underestimates,
in a region the world overlooks.
That taught me something early: **the best founders aren't the ones
with the most resources. They're the ones who refuse to accept the ceiling.**

I'm a founder, a builder, and an obsessive thinker
at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence, capital, and systemic change.**

I don't write code to ship features.
I write it to understand the machines that will reshape civilization —
and to make sure the people building them aren't all in the same three zip codes.

I care about **long-term games, first-principles thinking,**
and the kind of work that still matters in 20 years.

## What I'm Building

**Obelysk** is my life's current bet.

We're building **Small Language Models (SLMs)** —
not because large models aren't impressive,
but because the world doesn't run on data centers.
It runs on edges, constraints, and real conditions.
We're building AI that works *there.*

But Obelysk is just the beginning.

Alongside it, I'm architecting **HAIKU** — an AI-powered platform
that reimagines how people in LATAM learn and grow.
I'm building **70Y7**, a venture accelerator to make sure
the next generation of deep-tech founders in this region
doesn't have to figure it out alone like I did.
And I'm laying the groundwork for **RODGMONT Society** —
a long-horizon capital structure designed to compound
not just wealth, but impact, for decades.

This isn't a portfolio. It's a system.
Every piece is designed to reinforce the others —
capital funding research, research producing products,
products funding the next round of builders.

*I'm not building a startup. I'm building an ecosystem.*
